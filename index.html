THE PROJECT

The Concept

The Super Apes Club is a collection of 2222 unique hand-crafted super apes. What makes super apes different? It’s the character. We’re here to represent people who can express themselves via primal, alpha, and powerful super apes. It’s not about space, it’s not about boredom – it’s about the primal alpha energy!

The Community

We are all about utility. Our project is to bring together like-minded people that have a never-give-up attitude. If you’re a dark horse – that has come through to the top against all odds – then you’re a super ape! And, with it comes an exclusive set of utilities that you can check out within our roadmap section.

FAQs

What is a Super Ape?

Super Apes are randomly generated, non-fungible tokens that live on the Ethereum blockchain. A total of 2222 Super Apes have been created.

How do I buy a Super Ape?

You can get one by connecting your Ethereum wallet and minting.

Is this just an avatar?

The SAC is a club for those that have made it against all odds. The dark horse – the superhuman. Our concept is all about community – and helping people belong to something that they all can bond over.

What about marketing campaigns?

We are in this for the long haul. Our plans include dedicated PR campaigns, social media ad campaigns, billboards, and a lot more! We will not stop after the first collection – we’ll work on building Enchancted SAC and beyond! We’ll be announcing a lot more plans in the coming future!

Who is the designer of the Super Apes?

The Super Apes have been designed by Luis, known for his Dragon Ball Z fan art and crowded Comic-Con booths.
